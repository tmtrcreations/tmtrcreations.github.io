Custom electronics and prototyping.

This is TMTR Creations. We are a small company based in Natick, MA that specializes in custom electronics and rapid prototyping. Check out some of our projects below and on our Projects page.

  • Custom Electronics

    We can design custom electronics for your special purposes. We specialize in embeded Linux designs; especially products designed around the Raspberry Pi Compute Modules.

  • 3D Printing

    We can work with you to design and print custom 3D designs. We specialize in custom enclosures and products for the home. We mostly print in PLA but have the ability to print other materials.

  • Rapid Prototyping

    We can take your idea from idea to prototype. By combining our experience with electronic design and 3D design we can take your idea from a sketch on a piece of paper to a product prototype you can hold in your hand quickly while incorporating your feedback along the way.
