About Us


We are a small company based in Natick, MA that specializes in custom electronics and rapid prototyping. Our office is part of a collaborative workspace where we have access to an electronics station, 3D printers, CNC machines, and a laser cutter. We have the tools and experience to bring nearly and project to life.
